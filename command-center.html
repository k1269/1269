Battle Protocols

General Protocol

11:30 UTC ALL PLAYERS MUST BE IN 69S AND READY TO TELEPORT TO THEIR SPOTS!

Battle Start: All 17 players named below will be in APX to secure the first occupation by solo attacking with best heroes at 5/2/3 and pressing 2000 gem speedups until inside!

The moment castle is secured, leaders assigned to 69S will switch alliances.

Enemy Counter Strategy

Enemy will split into 2 alliances and run a counter rally.

  • Step 1: Double rally from 69S with Pale King, Barahir.
  • Step 2: Observe enemy alliances to see signs of counter rally. Enemy may disband rallies. Keep watching.
  • Step 3: As soon as counter starts, single rally from APX with Jamous.
  • Step 4: Observe enemy alliances to see signs of a second counter rally. Enemy may disband rallies. Keep watching.
  • Step 5: As soon as counter starts, double rally from 69S with Westley, Kai.
  • Step 6: Observe enemy alliances to see signs of a third counter rally. Enemy may disband rallies. Keep watching.
  • Step 7: As soon as counter starts, double rally from 69S with Alex, Fixoune.
  • Step 8: When castle is secured, initiate handover.

Castle Defense Procedures

  • Step 1: As soon as castle defense is lost Jamous, and joiners to head back with march speedups.
  • Step 2: Jamous, and joiners to start marching with best heroes at 5/2/3 timed to arrive 10-20 seconds after counter rallies land.
  • Step 3: Jamous to initiate countdown from 10 after speeding up his march. No more march speed ups by Jamous from that point and countdown continues. Joiners will try to match their arrival times to Jamous' countdown.
  • Step 4: 69S will empty the castle until rally lead, and 3 weak joiners remain inside.
  • Step 5: When Jamous says "1" rally lead leaves the garrison, leaving behind 3 joiner squads.
  • Step 6: Jamous captures the castle and joiners arrive right after.
  • Step 7: Jamous switches to defensive heroes.
  • Step 8: Joiners switch to 6/4/0 and garrison joiner heroes.
  • Step 9: Joiner heroes are shuffled by Hell to optimum combination.

Turret Operations

  • Step 1: Alex, Fixoune to double rally from 69S.
  • Step 2: Wait for 4 joiners to be inside the rally.
  • Step 3: An available turret garrison leader to join with best defensive set of heroes to Alex.
  • Step 4: As soon as turret is taken, Alex to hand over garrison to the rally leader and head back.
  • Step 5: Go back to step 1 till all 4 turrets are under control. Once all 4 is controlled, Fixoune to swap to defensive heroes and defend.
